Getting Started

⚠️ Connection Warning

Your PC is currently on a Public network profile. This usually blocks mobile connections. Switch your Wi-Fi to Private in Windows settings, or use the fix below.

Step 1 — Pair Your Phone

  1. Make sure your phone is on the same Wi-Fi as this PC.
  2. Click "+ Pair Mobile" on the Overview tab.
  3. Scan the QR code with your phone's camera app.
  4. Enter the 6-digit PIN shown in the sidebar.
  5. The Hub will show an approval popup — click Allow.

Step 2 — Connect an Agent PC

  1. On the Agent PC, run GestureLink_Agent.exe
  2. Both PCs must be on the same Wi-Fi network.
  3. On your phone, go to the Devices tab and tap Scan Network.
  4. The Agent PC will appear — tap Connect.
  5. Use the Trackpad or gesture camera to control the Agent PC.

Step 4 — Gesture Camera Control

  1. On your phone, go to the Control tab.
  2. Toggle the PC Camera switch ON.
  3. The Hub's camera will activate (visible in Overview).
  4. Use hand gestures in front of the camera to control the PC.
⚡ Tip: For best results, ensure good lighting and keep your hand 30–60cm from the camera.
